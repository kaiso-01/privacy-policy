What Information We Collect

Short answer: We do not collect or transmit personal information by default.

  • All task data (titles, descriptions, due dates/times, priorities, tags, and subtasks) is stored locally on your device only.
  • The app does not send your tasks or personal information to our servers or to any third parties unless you explicitly enable and authorize an optional cloud backup/sync feature (for example, Google Drive). If you enable Google Drive sync, the app will request permission via OAuth and may access your Drive to create and manage a backup file. We only access the files and metadata necessary to manage backups.
  • We do not collect analytics, device identifiers, or usage telemetry by default.

If you enable Google Sign-In (optional), we may request access to basic profile information (such as your email address) to associate backups with your account; this is optional and requested only with your consent.

If you believe any behavior contradicts this policy, please contact us at isslamkais40@gmail.com.

How We Collect Information

By default, My Tasks Reminder does not collect information. All data you create in the app is saved locally on your device. There is no automatic sending of task data to external servers.

If we add an optional cloud-sync or backup feature in the future, it will be explicitly described in the app and will require your opt-in consent before any data leaves your device.

Why We Process Any Data

Because My Tasks Reminder stores data locally and does not collect personal information by default, the primary reason any data is processed is to provide the core functionality of the app on your device (creating, storing, and reminding about tasks).

If you opt-in to a cloud backup feature in the future, the purpose and legal basis for that processing will be made clear before you enable it.

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Creating, storing, and managing your tasks and reminders
  • Sending you scheduled notifications for your tasks
  • Syncing your data across devices (if you enable this feature)
  • Displaying your tasks organized by date, priority, tags, and categories

We do NOT:

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

By default, all your task data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Encrypted Storage: We use platform-provided secure storage mechanisms
  • SQLite Database: Task data is stored in an encrypted local database
  • Secure Key Storage: Authentication credentials are stored using platform keychain/keystore

Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable cloud backup or sync, the data will be stored in the cloud storage account you choose (for example, your Google Drive account). Important points:

  • You explicitly authorize the app to access your chosen cloud account when enabling sync/backup (we use OAuth for Google Drive access).
  • The app stores an encrypted backup file in your cloud account; the file is created and managed within your Drive and is accessible only by you and any apps you authorize.
  • The app does not upload data to servers owned or controlled by the app developer. We do not view or access your task data on our own servers.
  • Data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS). Depending on your device settings, the data stored in your cloud account may be encrypted by the cloud provider.
  • Only you (with access to your cloud account) can access or decrypt your cloud-stored data. You may revoke the app's access to your cloud account at any time via your cloud provider's account settings.

Security Measures

We implement security measures including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest and in transit
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Compliance with mobile platform security guidelines

Important: While we implement robust security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services when you enable optional features:

The app only accesses files and metadata necessary to manage your backups. For details on how Google handles data, see: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Sign-In (Optional): If used to simplify authentication, we may request basic profile information such as your email address to map backups to your account. This is only requested with your explicit consent and is used solely to associate your backups with your account.

International Data Transfers

Short answer: We do not operate servers that receive or process your task data. By default, your data remains on your device in Algeria or wherever you use the app.

If you choose to back up or sync your data to a cloud provider, for example Google Drive, that backup file is stored in your cloud account and will be processed by the cloud provider according to their terms and policies.

  • Google Drive: When you enable Google Drive sync the app will request your explicit consent via Google's OAuth flow and will create/manage an encrypted backup file in your Drive. Google may store and process that file in locations outside Algeria (or outside your current country) in accordance with Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Other cloud providers: Your chosen provider may likewise transfer or process data in other jurisdictions according to its own policies and legal frameworks.

Important details:

  • Any transfer to a cloud provider is initiated by you and requires your explicit consent and authorization via OAuth.
  • The app developer does not transfer, receive, or host your task data on developer-controlled servers unless you explicitly enable a feature that states otherwise in-app.
  • You can review and revoke the app's access to your cloud account at any time via your cloud provider's account settings.
  • If you have concerns about international transfers by your chosen cloud provider, review that provider's privacy policy and terms and contact them for details about where and how they process data.
